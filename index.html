    About Me

    I am currently in the job market. If you are interested in my work please feel free to contact me.
    From March 2022 until January 2023, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Combinatorics groups at Bar-Ilan University, working with Ron Adin and Yuval Roichman. In December 2021, I received my PhD in mathematics at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens under the supervision of Christos Athanasiadis.
    Research Interests
    Symmetric and Quasisymmetric functions, permutation enumeration and combinatorial representation theory
    Unimodal, log-concave, real-rooted and gamma-positive polynomials arising in algebra, combinatorics and geometry
    Face enumeration of (subdivisions of) polytopes
    Combinatorics of real and complex reflection groups
